Framework of Skills for Inquiry Learning


Welcome to the FOSIL website! This is a tool for learning and will guide you through the FOSIL inquiry process.


This tool for learning will show you the FOSIL cycle. First you will have to put the different stages in the circular boxes that you think they match to. Then you will place their 'definitions' in the rectangular boxes. You can then move them if you change your mind.


Don't worry if you don't understand this it will all become clear soon!
